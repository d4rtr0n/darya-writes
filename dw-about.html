I am a child of the 90s and a first generation Iranian-Canadian. I was born and raised in Niagara Falls, Ontario, Canada. Living in Canada was great, and the winters were my favourite, but at 23 I decided I wanted to experience life abroad. So I moved to Amsterdam, The Netherlands. I planned only to stay for a couple of years to pursue a Masters Degree, but then I met the love of my life.

A year after moving across the Atlantic, I was diagnosed with Leukemia and my life was turned upside down. After intensive chemotherapy and a stem cell transplantation, I am now cancer free. That's right, I kicked cancer's a**.

I am a daughter, sister, wife-to-be, friend. I am a writer. I love purple, Hello Kitty, pizza, and donuts. I am a York University graduate. I am a Communication Scientist. I am learning how to navigate life in a foreign land, and I guess that makes me an expat. I am a Netflix binge watcher and social media addict.
